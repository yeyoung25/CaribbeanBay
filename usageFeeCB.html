  • Preferential Information
    Preference according to age (Present ID or documents that can confirm age)
    - Seniority: 65 years of age or older
    - Child : 36 months ~ 12 years old
    - Less than 36 months: free use
    Preferential treatment for the disabled
    - You can purchase a one-day pass at a preferential price after presenting your disability registration card
    - Severe degree of disability: person + 1 accompanying perso
    - Disabled person with less severe disability: himself/herself
    Persons of national merit Preferential price Preferential price
    - Present proof documents
    - Can be purchased at a preferential price for a limited-use ticket
    Preferential price for families with 3 or more children
    - Present proof of family with 3 or more children, such as health insurance card and certified copy
    - Preferential treatment: Couples with three or more children and direct children with the youngest child under the age of 15
    - Benefits apply even when only some family members in the household use it
