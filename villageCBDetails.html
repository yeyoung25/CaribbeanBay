VILLAGE

Spa Village

Enjoy a spa with your loved ones in a cozy and separate space. Relieve your stress and enjoy a pleasant atmosphere

Aquatic Cabana

Armed with a fantastic view and pleasantly restful atmosphere, indoor ondol cabana for four seasons is grand open!

Wave Village

You can relax your mind and body while enjoying the gentle roars of waves. Take a good rest for a while before heading to the wave pool!

Castle Village

A high-end royal sweet castle village built inside the splendid fortress. A perfect place for a special getaway for multi-family get-togethers. There is a lazy pool in full view.

River Way Village

This Village is close to the lazy pool area and has two parasols with two comfortable beach chairs.

Cabana Village

This Village surrounded by lazy pool is located right in the middle of Caribbean Bay, in close proximity to the Wave Pool, Tube rides and Aquatic Center.

San Juan Village

Enjoy pleasant leisure in the pirate village full of vitality. Located in the middle of wild river zone, it offers convenient access to restaurants and exciting attractions.

Slide Village

You have to be strong enough to conquer all slides at the Caribbean Bay. Take a short rest at the village located on a hill where a cool breeze gently blows.

Kids Village

This Village has a cozy atmosphere with not much traffic around. Its convenient location close to the Kids' Pool is perfect as a resting place for kid and parents.

Adventure Village

An excellent place to enjoy Caribbean Bay to the full. An adventure pool with a skeleton-shaped bucket pouring down water, with fun all around.

Surfing Village

The perfect place for lovers of surfing seeking adventure and thrills. Refresh yourself before taking a surfing on waves.

Raft Village

This Village is close to the lazy pool area and has two parasols with two comfortable beach chairs.

Have fun while overlooking a wild river! Exciting attractions like Tower Boomerango, Tower Raft and Wild Blaster and a number of great restaurants are conveniently located nearby!

Tower Village

Visit Aquatic Daybed if you cool off heat after exploring water adventures at the Caribbean Bay. Enjoy a comfortable and peaceful atmosphere surrounded by the dense forest.

Aquatic Daybed

Lie down on a soft round bed in a cozy indoor space and enjoy a relaxing break!

Wild Lounge

Playing in the open wild river pool Lie down on the sofa and enjoy your relaxing day.
