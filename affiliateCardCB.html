AFFILIATE CARD

Guidance on using affiliated cards
  • - When paying with affiliated cards, the standard of interpersonal rate is applied.
  • - It is limited to payment by the card holder once a day based on the date of visit.
  • - It is applied when the terms and conditions for each card are met.
  • - Depending on the card company and card type, it may not be possible to make online reservations or to check whether preferential treatment is available (fulfillment of usage records).
  • - The billing discount card is applied when paying on-site at Everland. Please check with each card company for details.
  • - It takes 3 to 5 days to reflect credit card charge cancellation (excluding weekends/holidays)
  • - Affiliate card discounts do not overlap with other discounts.
  • - Mobile cards cannot be used for on-site payment.
  • - For cards other than those listed below, please check the card issuer's website.

For detailed terms and conditions, such as Samsung Card additional services, please check the Samsung Card website.
