RESTAURANT

La Costa

Enjoy simple one-dish foods and sets like udon and fried rice

Beach Side Bar

At the Peach Side Bar, where you can enjoy a relaxing, Meet cocktails with autumn colors, fragrant coffee, and delicious snacks.

Beachside Bar is a must-visit if you've visited the Caribbean Beach Village!

Paul Bassett

At the outdoor wave pool in Caribbean Bay with exotic landmarks and sculptures, Enjoy Paul Bassett's coffee and food, famous for ice cream lattes.

Harbor Master

Caribbean Seaside Village Local Cuisine!

If you want to feel the true Marcaribe, go to Harbor Master full of Mexican food.

Madrid

Caribbean Bay at a glance! Eat meals in a cozy ambience on an outdoor terrace. Various and delicious meals will improve your satisfaction.
