THEME STORY

The Caribbean Sea,first discovered by Christopher Columbus in 1492

The Caribbean Sea, first discovered by Christopher Columbus in 1492, was famous for its pirates who flocked there due to the abundant natural resources to be found, such as gold and silver.. The Caribbean Bay is built based on the theme of the exotic and luxurious aspects of those buccaneering times. The Caribbean Bay has unique features, containing both European and American culture, and consists of 5 areas, the Aquatic Center, Sea Wave, Bay Slide, Fortress and Wild River. The following are the main features of each zone:

  • AQUATIC CENTER

    Romantic seas shores that greet me all year round! Enjoy the exciting adventure and comfortable rest in the midst of exotic atmosphere of the Caribbean.

  • SEA WAVE

    Sea Wave! The magnificent Caribbean sky and the cool white sand of the beaches beckon anyone to come and enjoy their vacation in paradise.
    Explore the natural beauty and crystal clear waters at Caribbean Bay

  • BAY SLIDE

    Bay Slide! Unstoppable excitement and expectation! Experience outrageous speeds and water slides! Incredibly fun and exciting adventures begin here.

  • FORTRESS

    Fortress! A world of joy unfolding within the magnificence of a giant castle,
    This adventure Is one of laughter all around, to be remembered later as precious family memories.

  • WILD RIVER

    Wild River ! No thrill goes beyond this! An amazing action fantasy beings when a notorious pirate makes known his evil intention.
    Experience the ultimate adventure!
